How to Play!

How to play Divide Them Away!


Movement

Movment in Divide Them Away is done using the WASD keys.
You can point your character in a certain direction by moving your mouse in that direction.


Shooting

Shooting is done through the left mouse button.

Switch weapons with Q and E


Enemy Types

Shooting Platform

Moves around the screen...watch where they're facing!

Sinosodal

A pulsating sinosodal beast! It's best to just avoid them.

Missiles

They'll chase you to no end. Destroy them before they become a problem!

StraightLiners

Dodge or destroy, it's up to you.
